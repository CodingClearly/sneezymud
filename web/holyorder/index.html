Welcome to the online home of the Brotherhood of Galek!

The Brotherhood is a faction on GrimhavenMUD (formerly SneezyMUD) and is based in the fair city of Brightmoon.


First and foremost, this is a place to gather information, the Archives section of this site will hopefully become a large source of knowledge pertaining to the histories and The World of Grimhaven.


Also, it is a place for the members of the Brotherhood to get information about the faction, and about their responsibilities. Important notices from Vangard and other members will also be put up here.


As of now, the Brotherhood and the other factions of Grimhaven are still getting organized, so the information yet compiled will likely be meagre.


Thanks for visiting! Enjoy.

-Silvern Z'Tharnin, Brotherhood Apostle of Internal Communications
