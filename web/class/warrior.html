What's a Warrior Anyway?

Warriors are characters highly trained in the art of war and how to fight. They typically desire high strengths as well as high constitutions.

They are skilled in the use of weapons and armor and possess many offensive combat abilities, both armed and unarmed. Their propensity to dish out as well as take damage is unequaled.

Warrior Disciplines

Warriors have the following skill disciplines:
Adventuring Skills, Hand to Hand Abilities, Brawling Skills, Warrior Abilities, Slash Specialization, Blunt Specialization, Pierce Specialization.

The warrior discipline is strictly for those of the warrior class. The warrior discipline can not be trained until tactics is half learned. Warriors are required to have 1.67% learning in this discipline for every level over 5th.
