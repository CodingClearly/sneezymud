Newbie Guide: SneezyMUD's Rental System

This page last modified: 12-13-97

SneezyMUD is presently experimenting with a new rent scheme. Please read HELP RENT for the latest information.

Our present goals with regards to the rent system:

  1. A balanced economy such that rent cost is not necessary for draining money from The World.

  2. A rent charge in-line with a realistic examination of what services an inn provides.

  3. A system which does not penalize a player for NOT playing.

  4. A system that does not need hands-on management in the event of unforeseen down times.
