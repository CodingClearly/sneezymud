FAQ: How do I increase my deities' potency?

This page last modified: 12-13-97

Your deities' potency is related to the performance of all members of that faction. For instance, as you do well (killing the enemies of the faction and aiding your allies), you not only raise your faction percentage, but also raise your deities potency. Similarly, potency will drop if you allow your faction percentage to drop. Realize though, faction percentage is specific to you, while all members of a faction contribute to the potency.

Potency is drained somewhat each time the deity interferes in the mortal realms. The most common way this happens is in the regeneration of piety to that faction's clerics, but Miracles and the like also have their effect on potency.
