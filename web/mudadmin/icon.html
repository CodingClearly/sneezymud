Resolutions of the Immortal Council


1: **RESOLVED**
All members of the ICon have a single vote which they may cast.

2: **RESOLVED**
All votes of the ICon are to be submitted and responded to via email.

3: **RESOLVED**
A vote is to be called by the OpMan, a resolution is drafted, and the
resolution is submitted to all current members of the ICon. The voting
period extends for 24 hours after the resolution is submitted to all
members. If a vote fails to receive the necessary quorum of votes to
consider it valid, the vote may be extended for an additional 24 hours
at the discretion of the OpMan.


4: **RESOLVED**
A vote of the ICon is only considered valid if 1/2 of the current
members cast a vote. Any vote where fewer than 1/2 of the current
members vote may not be considered valid. Lack of participation
(resulting in an inability to establish a working system) will result in
a removal of quorum requirements.


5: **RESOLVED**
All votes of the ICon are assumed to be decided by majority decision of
those people casting votes.

6: **RESOLVED**
Resolved resolutions will be posted on a webpage housed on the spasm
webserver for review by immortals as well as mortals.


7: **RESOLVED**
The majority of discussion of issues shall be conducted by way of the
ICon mailing listserv housed on the spasm machine.


8: **RESOLVED**
Meetings of the ICon shall take place upon request of 5 or more members
of the ICon.


9: **RESOLVED**
Future OpMan selection will be made by members of the ICon by anonymous
ballot.


10: **RESOLVED**
A position known as JrOpMan shall be created.

11: **RESOLVED**
The position of JrOpMan shall retain a immortal character level of 56.

12: **Undecided**
The immortal known as Moath shall be selected as JrOpMan.

13: **Undecided**
The immortal known as Finch shall be selected as JrOpMan.

14: **RESOLVED**
The JrOpMan will act as OpMan in the event of a vacancy in the OpMan
position until such time that a vote can occur to fill the vacant OpMan
position.


15: **RESOLVED**
A Mortal council shall be formed and shall be known as MoCon.


16: **RESOLVED**
The Mortal Council shall be made up from 2 player characters from each
of the races and must include 1 high level (25-50) as well as 1 low
level (5-25) represenative for each race.


17: **RESOLVED**
The Mortal Council shall have at least 1 represenative from each class.


18: **Failed**
Members of the Mortal Council shall be appointed by the OpMan, JrOpMan,
and one additional member of the ICon.


19: **Failed**
Members of the Mortal Council shall be elected by the players of
Grimhaven.


20: **Undecided**
The OpMan and one represenative of the ICon will preside over each
Mortal Council Meeting.


21: **Undecided**
The Mortal Council will have meetings on a weekly basis.


22: **RESOLVED**
The immortal level of 52 shall be included in the ICon. This inclusion
will be by voice only and the level 52 member of the ICon will not have
a valid vote in the ICon.


23: **Failed**
The immortal level of 52 shall be included in the ICon as a voting
member.


24: **Failed**
The immortal level of 52 shall not be included in the ICon.


25: **Failed**
All votes by the ICon shall be made in an open forum on the ICon mailing
list. On more sensitive issues the OpMan retains the right to call an
anonymous ballot sent only to the OpMan.



26: **Failed**
All votes by the ICon shall be made in an anonymous ballot sent only to
the OpMan.


27: **Failed**
All votes by the ICon shall be made in an open forum on the ICon mailing
list.


28: **RESOLVED**
Players who reach the 50th level shall be offered the option of having
thier character "immortalized" in the world of Grimhaven. Players
accepting this option must retire the player character "immortalized".
("immortalized" defined: to have a Mobile [NPC] made in the likeness of
the player character.)


29: **Failed**
It shall be recommended to the mudadmin that the former immortal known
as Darkanan shall be reinstated in his position at the level of 54.


30: **RESOLVED**
Members of the ICon may request the OpMan call a vote on a
resolution, and it falls upon the OpMan to accept this request and put a
vote before the ICon, or to decline to vote on the requested resolution.
If the OpMan declines to call a vote on a resolution, a member of the
ICon may draft a resolution, and upon it being seconded by another
member of the ICon, a vote on that resolution is called.


31: **RESOLVED**
Immortals will be allowed to use and rent limited items with their mortal characters. This use will be restricted to items that have not reached their max exist. Automatic reposession (code supported) will occur at such time as an item reaches the max allowed.


32: **RESOLVED**
The ICon will seek a LOW approved volunteer to review and revise the current max exists.
