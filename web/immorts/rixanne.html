Rixanne has been playing Sneezy for six years in various characters, with her proudest accomplishment in achieving the mid-40th levels on her tri-class warrior/mage/cleric.  Rixanne recently retired, but served
Sneezy as a skills programmer.  She also founded and served as the Chair of the Player Balance Committee.
For work, she is the systems administrator for a small department at an even smaller community college located
in the county of San Diego, California.  For fun, she gets together with other local Sneezyites to burn meat,
shoot pool and compare beer labels.
