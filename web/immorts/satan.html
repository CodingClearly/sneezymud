Not much is known about Satan anymore since he has rarely been seen around Sneezy as of late. The only picture of him in the SneezyWeb Team's possession is also quite out of date (something to the order of 6+ years old), and apparently there is no updated picture forthcoming. Due to the lack of sufficient visual excitement, the above link contains some humorous content along the lines of what Satan would find hilarious (DISCLAIMER: SneezyMUD is in no way associated with AfroSquad). What IS known about Satan is that he has been around since v2.5, and has perpetuated a cycle of having a strong presence for months at a time on the MUD and then fading into obscurity. Rumored to have constructed a healthy chunk of the world, he has earned the moniker 'The Maker' (rumors also have it that his contributions are greatly exagerated *bahahaha*). Notoriously lazy and obnoxious, somehow he has managed to maintain an Immortal on SneezyMUD - who knows, perhaps he will regain his motivation and charge onto the scene again (current status: funktified).
