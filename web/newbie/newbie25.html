Newbie Guide: Official Policy: storage characters

This page last modified: 12-13-97

The use of storage characters is ILLEGAL and grounds for character deletion or other punishment.

A storage character is defined as any character created for the expressed purposes of skirting the rules on rent or equipment sharing. This includes, but is not limited to:

  1. A character given a maximum karma score to purchase items.

  2. Switching equipment between pc's whether directly or indirectly.

  3. A character used to hang onto an item until some other character needs it.
