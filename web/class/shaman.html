What's a Shaman Anyway?

Shamans are practitioners of a few certain types of magic. Mostly loners, they have specialized in elements that provide a measure of control over their environment.

They have developed over time a relationship with the darker spirits and are familar with the governing laws of the undead. They can extract magic elixers from the earth and items they find around them to encapsulate the magic of their flashier mage cousins.

Like porcupines, their self defense has used the power of their attackers to protect themselves. Their relationship with the undead and the darker arts has given them an understanding of the life force in each being and has allowed shaman to develop ways to drain such life forces. Not surprisingly, they also have developed the ability to restore some life force although in a slow fashion using touch and communion.

Shaman Disciplines

Shaman have the following basic disciplines:
Combat Skills, Magic Lore, Shaman Abilities, Adventuring Skills, Pierce Specialization, Blunt Specialization, Wizardry

Shaman have the following advanced skill disciplines:
Fighting Skills, Alchemy

Shaman have the following advanced spell disciplines:
Draining, Healing, Undead Control, Totemism
