Newbie Guide: Rules for playing multi-characters on SneezyMUD

This page last modified: 12-13-97

SneezyMUD allows you to make and play as many characters and classes as you like. However, you are only allowed to have ONE of these characters connected to the mud at any given time.

There are NO exceptions. This one PC rule applies to characters in the inn and link dead characters. You are not allowed to switch characters by dropping the link of one of them. You must find an inn and rent your character before logging another on line. Storage characters and equipment sharing are both illegal so it is no excuse to say that you are just switching gear. Your characters are not allowed to share equipment in anyway whether such sharing is direct or indirect, or temporary or permanent. Equipment sharing is not allowed in any form between your characters.

We realize that this means that, unlike other unrelated PCs, your PCs won't meet each other like all other unrelated characters. We also recognize that unrelated characters do sell and give equipment to each other. However, in the interest of consistency and enforceability, your PCs will not be able to transfer equipment between them. You can NOT have two characters on line at the same time or share your equipment between characters no matter what method you use to transfer the equipment.

In addition, using one character to assist another indirectly is also prohibited. This includes but is not limited to doing corpse retrievals, or dropping equipment, logging in a second character and picking it up.

All gods will enforce this rule impartially but firmly. Players found violating this policy will have actions taken against them. These actions range from equipment deletion to account deletion and site ban depending on the circumstances of the violation.
