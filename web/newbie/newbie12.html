Newbie Guide: Character Stats and What They Mean

This page last modified: 12-13-97

Character Statistics

SneezyMUD distinguishes between a player's NATURAL and their ADJUSTED stats. Natural stats are those stats set by you at the outset of character development. Natural stats have a slight fluctuation based on the age of the character. Adjusted stats are what the person's actual ability is after being modified for magical affects. The most important difference between adjusted and natural stats is that events with a long-term effect (such as gaining) rely strictly on the natural stat, while short-term effects (skills, spells) will reference the adjusted stat.

Stat Summary:

  • Strength affects your ability to manipulate weight and your combat damage.
  • Dexterity affects volume manipulation and offensive skill abilities.
  • Brawn affects your ability to wear armor and your hardiness.
  • Constitution affects your endurance and your life force.
  • Agility affects your defensive combat abilities.
  • Intelligence affects your maximum total learning.
  • Focus affects the success rate of your skills.
  • Wisdom affects how fast you will learn your skills.
  • Perception affects your abilities to see and evaluate.
  • Karma affects your luck.
  • Charisma affects your ability to lead others including control pets.
  • Speed affects how fast you are able to do things.
