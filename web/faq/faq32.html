FAQ: What's with all these pieces of metal I keep finding?

This page last modified: 12-13-97

The various creatures of SneezyMUD are no longer restricted purely to using and owning talens to reflect their personal wealth. Various commodities have been obtained by some of them and you may soon find them for yourself as you explore.

These commodities are considered raw materials and have little worth by themselves. No shopkeeper will accept them as currency. But, they do have value to certain peoples as they are useful commodities. The raw materials are used in certain spells and skills as well as by the repair shops. As such, a commodities market has been created to trade specifically in these materials. You can sell the metals that you obtain, or buy what the markets have for sale for your own purposes.

As you might expect, the better and more expensive metals are harder to come by and additionally certain creatures have a greater likelihood of having certain metals than others.
