FAQ: How come my stats aren't what I chose initially?

This page last modified: 12-13-97

(stat flux)

This question is commonly asked by players after they view their stats with the ATTRIBUTE command. The short answer to this question is that they are. The real answer is that the stats you set initially are used as a baseline. However, a slight variation from this baseline is implemented based on the player's age. For instance, a younger PC will have increased strength and karma, but lower intelligence and wisdom, while more elderly PC's essentially have the reverse of this.
