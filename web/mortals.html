Below you will find a list of just a few of SneezyMUD's players. If you are a player of SneezyMUD and would like to be added here please send me you name, major PC names, email address (if you would like it listed), web address if you have a personal page, and a short bio of yourself including things like where you go to school or work, hobbies, interests, etc. Many people enjoy finding out more about the people they adventure with so a nice paragraph about yourself is always welcome. Also if you have access to a scanner and would like to email me a scanned image of yourself please feel free to do so. Any format and any size is fine (please use a fairly high resolution and DPI setting for the scan) I have utilities to adjust whatever else I need. If you do not have a scanner on hand and would like to snailmail me a picture I would be more then happy to scan it and return the picture to you.
