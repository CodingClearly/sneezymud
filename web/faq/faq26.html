FAQ: Ack, I lost all my goods, what's up with that?

This page last modified: 12-13-97

Refer to HELP REIMBURSE for starters. In general, dying just before the game crashed or rebooted is valid reason to get a reimbursement. If the game did not crash, then HELP LOSING LINK probably explains your situation.

In both cases, you will need a 54+ level god. WHO -GL to see who's on.

The other obvious alternative is that you couldn't afford your rent and the rent contingencies as detailed in HELP RENT occurred.
